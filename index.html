USAGE:
    lyrs [FLAGS] <SEARCH>...

FLAGS:
    -h, --help       Prints help information
    -l, --login      Login in with a genius account
    -v, --version    Prints version information

ARGS:
    <SEARCH>...    Search for a song like (i.e) Slipknot Duality
